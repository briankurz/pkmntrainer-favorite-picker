Picker

Last updated: May 27, 2025

This website is a version of the favorite pokemon picker but for trainers, created because the one I found hasn't been updated since 2019, so it's missing everyone from Hisui and Paldea. All character images are arbitrarily chosen by me, and more features may come later. All credit for the template of this favorite picker goes to the original creators.

For each group of trainers presented to you, click one or more of your favorites from that group and press the "Pick" button. Eventually, your favorite trainers will start appearing under "Found Favorites". You can continue as long as you like to construct an arbitrarily long list of your favorite trainers.
